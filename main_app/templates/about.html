About

Flowers have always brought me so much joy. Growing up, my Mom and Grandfather would spend a lot of time outside in the flower garden or vegetable garden, playing in the dirt. I didn't realize then how much hard work it takes to grow, maintain, and care for plants. Now, I love planting flowers in my own garden and in flower pots all along the back deck. The more colorful the flowers, the better!

If you like flowers, have your own beauties, or just want to poke around please come join us! I'd love to see what you've got growing on!
